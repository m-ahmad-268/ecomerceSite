Description

This new biker jacket reinterprets the tailored feel of virgin wool in a bold, casual way. The classic menswear silhouette, recognizable by the wide lapel, the side zipper closure and the epaulettes, is enhanced by the shiny monili details that illuminate the loops at the bottom, completing the garment in a sophisticated way.

Materials

A material of timeless elegance, the virgin wool fabric features a very fluid hand and a smooth, compact texture. A silk-blend crêpe lining completes the garment and provides added comfort.

Our items are manufactured in the hamlet of Solomeo, in Italy, respecting human beings and labor dignity and in harmony with the land.
